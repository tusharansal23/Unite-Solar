About Us

Who We Are

Unite Solar is an unlisted private company incorporated on 21st September, 2016. Unite Solar is a Renewable Energy Service Company (RESCO), we are one of the leading Solar Energy system developers & integrators Potential directors with regional knowledge at Unite Solar can handle varied customers throughout India. The company is devoted to its quality and safety policies, creates enormous value for its customers in terms of innovative and energy efficient engineering, prompt completion & notable after sales support.

We provide turnkey solutions from concept to commissioning of Solar Projects. The company understands the growing demand of Green Power in local and global markets and hence ensures energy efficiency as one of the highest offerings. We have a smart young team of professionals with experience in the field of Design to Commissioning.

OUR MISSION: Our mission is to design, build, and manage world-class renewable energy solutions that empower our customers with innovative, sustainable, and reliable energy options tailored to their unique needs.

 

OUR VISION: Our vision is to become a leading provider of 20 TWh (Terawatt hours) of clean energy, leveraging advanced technologies in power generation and energy storage to deliver cost-effective, sustainable, and adaptable solutions for our customers.

Why Unite Solar ?

We are dedicated to designing, installing, and maintaining affordable solar solutions for commercial and residential purposes

Unite Solar have established its presence across four major southern states of India viz., Tamilnadu, Karnataka, Kerala, Andhra Pradesh

With a diversified portfolio of more than 1 MW in southern India, Unite Solar possesses clear experience and expertise in setting up land based solar power plants for power utilities, large corporates and industrial establishments.
